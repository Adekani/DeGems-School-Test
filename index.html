Our Story & Vision

Building a legacy of excellence since 2005.

A Tradition of Excellence

DeGems International School was founded on the belief that every child is a gem with a unique brilliance waiting to be polished. We provide a learning environment where discipline meets creativity and where young minds develop the courage to lead.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a community known for academic excellence, innovation, and strong moral standards.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, student-centered education that builds character, responsibility, and leadership.

Our Vision

To raise disciplined, confident, and globally competent learners who can thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Academic Excellence

Blending the Nigerian National Curriculum with global best practices.

Early Years (Creche & Nursery)

We focus on learning-through-play, developing social skills, and early literacy. Our environment is safe, colorful, and designed to stimulate curiosity in our youngest gems.

  • ● Sensory Play & Exploration
  • ● Basic Numeracy
  • ● Creative Arts

Primary School

Foundational literacy & numeracy take center stage, supported by science, cultural studies, and digital competence. We encourage independent reading and critical thinking.

  • ● Cambridge Primary Checkpoint Prep
  • ● ICT & Coding Basics
  • ● French Language

Secondary School

Deeper specialization occurs here. Students engage in rigorous STEM programs, humanities, and leadership training. We prepare them thoroughly for WAEC, NECO, and IGCSE examinations.

  • ● Advanced Science Labs
  • ● Robotics & Programming
  • ● Debate & Public Speaking
